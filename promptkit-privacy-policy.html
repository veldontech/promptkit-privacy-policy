01 ‚Äî Overview

Overview & Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how Veldon Technologies ("we," "our," or "us") handles information in connection with PromptKit, a Google Chrome browser extension ("the Extension"). This policy applies to all versions of PromptKit distributed through the Chrome Web Store and any other official channels.

PromptKit is a productivity tool that allows you to save, search, manage, and inject AI prompts into websites such as ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. The Extension is designed to operate entirely within your browser, without any communication to servers owned or operated by Veldon Technologies.

By installing or using PromptKit, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with its terms, please uninstall the Extension and discontinue use.

03 ‚Äî Data Storage

How Your Data Is Stored

All data you create within PromptKit ‚Äî including saved prompts, tags, titles, and settings ‚Äî is stored exclusively using the Chrome Storage API, a browser-native mechanism provided by Google Chrome.

üì¶ Local Storage ‚Äî What This Means for You

Your data lives only on your own device, inside your browser's local storage. It is not uploaded to any server, cloud service, or third-party platform.

Veldon Technologies has no access to, and cannot retrieve, view, modify, or delete any data stored by the Extension on your device.

If you uninstall PromptKit, all locally stored data will be permanently removed from your browser in accordance with Chrome's standard extension removal process.

Because storage is local to your device and browser profile, your prompts are not automatically synced across devices unless Chrome's built-in profile sync is enabled by you separately. Even in that case, sync occurs through your personal Google account infrastructure ‚Äî not through any system controlled by Veldon Technologies.
